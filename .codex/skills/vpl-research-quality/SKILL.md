# VPL Research Quality

Follow this workflow for every research task.

## 1) Lock Decision Question

- Define the single decision this research supports.
- Define measurable success criteria before data collection.
- Define minimal sample and channels needed.

## 2) Collect Comparable Evidence

- Use the same prompt/script per participant group.
- Keep capture format structured and consistent.
- Separate observed facts from interpretation.

## 3) Analyze Transparently

- Aggregate quantitative metrics and key qualitative themes.
- Flag bias risks and data quality gaps.
- Tie conclusions directly to original decision question.

## 4) Verify With Gates

- Run the checklist in `references/quality-gates.md`.
- Verify issue acceptance criteria one by one.
- Validate all reported metrics against raw data.

## 5) Report Completion

- Provide recommendation: go, no-go, or pivot.
- Provide confidence level and top uncertainty.
- Map completed behavior directly back to issue criteria.
