# Research Quality Gates

## Question quality gates

- Keep one primary decision question per task.
- Define clear success thresholds before data collection.
- Avoid changing hypotheses after seeing results.

## Data collection gates

- Use consistent prompts across participants.
- Capture source, timestamp, and participant segment.
- Prevent duplicate entries in the dataset.

## Analysis gates

- Keep metrics reproducible from raw data.
- Distinguish fact, inference, and recommendation.
- Explicitly list data gaps and bias risk.

## Decision gates

- Provide a clear go/no-go/pivot outcome.
- Tie outcome to pre-defined thresholds.
- Document what changes next based on result.

## Communication gates

- Keep summaries concise and evidence-linked.
- Include one-page KPI snapshot when possible.
- Keep raw data access path documented.
